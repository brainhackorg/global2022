    What is Brainhack?

    Brainhack is a unique event format that convenes researchers from across the globe and a myriad of disciplines to work together on innovative projects related to neuroscience.

    Year after year, global Brainhack events have brought researchers together to participate in open collaboration, and regional Brainhack events keep the momentum going throughout the year.
